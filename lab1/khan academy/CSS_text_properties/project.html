Why I started to go gym

Mon 14.43 PM 30/01/23

I have many reasons why I started going to the gym. Probably the main reason is because I didn't want to be weak. They always told me that I was thin and weak. It made me very sad, but it also gave me strength. Since this was my motivation not to give up and move on. I'm glad I was teased like that because these people made me stronger

Why I never stop?
