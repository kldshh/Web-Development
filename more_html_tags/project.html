Pancakes with meat

  • Time: 1 hour 40 minutes
  • Serves: when it is hot

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Water

    • For filling:
  • Minced meat
  • Onion
  • Dried garlic
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Dried coriander
  • Vegetable oil
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 liter
Eggs 2 it.
Vegetable oil 7 spoons
Sugar 1 spoon
Salt 1 spoon
Soda 0.5 spoon
Minced meat 400 g
Onion 1 it.
Onion 0.5 pcs
Milk 1 liter

Step 1: Peel, wash and dice the onion.
Step 2: Pancakes with meat Preheat the frying pan. Pour vegetable oil. Lay out the onion. Fry over medium heat, stirring, until golden brown.
Step 3: Pancakes with meat Add the minced meat to the onion. Fry, stirring, until tender for about 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper. Mix it up. Cool.
Step 4: Pancakes with meat - Pour warm milk into a bowl. Break the eggs there as well. Add salt and sugar. Beat with a whisk or mixer. Add butter and flour, beat until smooth.
Step 5: Pancakes with meat - Boil the kettle. Pour soda into a glass, pour boiling water (3 tablespoons). Pour the soda into a bowl with the dough. Stir well. Let the dough stand for 10 minutes.
Step 6: Pancakes with meat Preheat the frying pan. Add 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil. Pour half a ladle of dough into the center of the pan. Spread the dough over the entire surface of the pan. As soon as the top side of the pancake is no longer liquid, turn the pancake over.
Step 7: Pancakes with meat Then turn over and bake on the other side for 30-40 seconds.
Step 8: Pancakes with meat So bake all the pancakes.
Step 9: Pancakes with meat Put the meat filling on the pancake, roll the pancake into an envelope. Pancakes with meat can be served.
Step 10: Pancakes with meat Pancakes with meat served with sour cream. Enjoy your meal!
